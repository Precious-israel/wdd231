About Edsarah

Founded on the principles of freshness, integrity, and innovation, Edsarah Confectionery and Supermart has grown from a humble bakery into one of Lagos’ most trusted food and retail brands. With branches spread across the metropolis, we deliver a unique combination of premium groceries, delightful confectioneries, and excellent customer experience.

Our Mission

To consistently deliver fresh, quality, and affordable products in a clean, friendly environment — creating joy in every bite and convenience in every cart.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s leading neighborhood supermart and bakery — blending local taste with world-class retail service and innovation.

Our Core Values

  • Freshness: We bake and stock daily to ensure the highest quality.
  • Integrity: Honesty and transparency guide every aspect of our business.
  • Customer-Centricity: Your satisfaction drives our decisions.
  • Innovation: We embrace change to improve your shopping experience.
  • Community: We are deeply rooted in giving back and supporting our local communities.

Meet Our Team

Our passionate team of bakers, store managers, customer service professionals, and logistics experts work together to ensure your satisfaction. From sunrise baking sessions to night-time deliveries, Edsarah is powered by a team that truly cares.

Come Visit Us

With multiple branches across Lagos, you’re never too far from a taste of Edsarah. Whether you’re craving fresh bread, stocking up for the week, or simply exploring, we can’t wait to welcome you.
